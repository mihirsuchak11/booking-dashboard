### Recommendation: **Start with Product-First Landing Page**

| Approach | Pros | Cons |
|----------|------|------|
| **Product Page First** âœ… | Faster to market, focused messaging, clear conversion goals | Need restructuring later when adding products |

- You need traction NOW before expanding
- One focused landing page converts better than split attention
- You can add a parent company site later (e.g., `yourcompany.com` â†’ `yourcompany.com/voiceagent`)

